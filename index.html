• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Instructions for making a grill cheese sandwitch

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread on frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for two minutes
